MISSION STATEMENT AND STRATEGIC PLAN FOR QUALITY IMPROVEMENT
DPS is committed to supporting all learners through a relevant, robust, and relationship-driven educational experience.
Our school district is on a journey for continuous improvement and an “On-going Tradition of Excellence.”
Our leadership team is involved in a quest to facilitate continuous improvement in areas such as: on-going
professional development, data driven decision making, customer focus, increased parental and
community involvement, teamwork, and superior communication through daily activities and best practices.
