Lead Investors are a committed group of Chamber members, whose significant
level of membership dues provides substantial support for the Chamber’s efforts
to improve the business climate. Lead Investors receive additional recognition
benefits throughout the year that include –
• Logo link on the Chamber’s website home page
• Annual recognition in the Chamber Business Directory
• Special sponsorship signage at Chamber events
• Reserved seating at Community Breakfasts and Legislative events
