C
ameron University-Duncan, a branch of Cameron University in Lawton, provides a top quality education close to home
for those in Stephens County and surrounding areas. Enrollment at CU-Duncan continues to grow, with approximately
1,400 students enrolled each year.
Several associate, bachelor’s and master’s degree programs may be completed exclusively on the Duncan campus. The campus also
offers many classes for additional degree programs that can be completed at the Cameron University main campus in Lawton.
The diverse student body at CU-Duncan includes first-time college students of all ages, adults returning to college for further
education, and high school juniors and seniors who qualify for college while completing their secondary education.
In addition, Cameron University guarantees the quality of its bachelor’s degrees. The “Cameron University Guarantee” is the
only program of its kind offered by a public university in Oklahoma and one of the few in the nation. The Guarantee is possible
because of the proven quality and demonstrated commitment of Cameron’s faculty and staff.
