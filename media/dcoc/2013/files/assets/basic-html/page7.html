n our present world of economic instability it is comforting to be a part
of a community that is supported by a Chamber of Commerce who
has a vision for our local economic stability and a strong determination
to make it happen.
Your Duncan Chamber of Commerce is led by CEO and President
Chris Deal. Our board of directors is a committed group of community
leaders who work closely with The City of Duncan, Main Street Duncan,
The Duncan Economic Development Foundation, and other community
based organizations to strengthen our economy and our future growth.
TheChamber’s perspective is consistent in promoting itsmembership
from the largest to the smallest to succeed in business and to promote quality of life. Our 480 plus
membership is made up of large and small businesses, non-profit organizations, and individuals who
corporately and individually play a major role in the success of our Chamber.
We want to encourage you to take advantage of the numerous programs and benefits offered by the
Chamber and to seize the opportunities provided by the Chamber to network and build relationships that
will directly benefit your business and the success of our Chamber and economic stability.
I want to express my appreciation for the opportunity to serve as your 2013 Chairman of the Board
and to thank you for your personal involvement and commitment to the Duncan Chamber of Commerce.
Sincerely,
Jerry Shaffer,
2013 Chairman of the Board
